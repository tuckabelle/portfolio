ABOUT

Based in Washington, D.C., I am a web designer and developer seeking a full-time job in the District.
Check out my resumé here!

After years of studying and practicing Art and aesthetic design, I began to gravitate more and more towards the web. I became invested in projects and clients, determined that functionality and structure evolve into something truly effective and maintainable; simply working with Photoshop didn’t seem as fulfilling as it once did. I found absolute joy in crafting code that worked and was always excited to go back to old scripts to implement newly discovered methods.

Believe you me, I was surprised as anyone that the hours passed fiddling with scripts and combing through website experiences flew by just as fast as when I was painting or playing guitar or, well, Art-ing.
